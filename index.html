About me

Ambitious, versatile, and reliable team-worker adaptable to any challenge with the ability to build key relationships and communicate well with stakeholders. Having left school with excellent grades, a wide range of skills and a passion in computer science, I wish to pursue a role in software as I believe it is the future.


I am currently enrolled on the T Level Digital Production, Design and development where I have studied all things computer related and I have gained almost a year of work experience so far (400 hrs) at Sunderland City Council. During my time on the course it has given me the technical skills needed in the workplace and allowed me to explore both my passions: solving problems and developing creative solutions.


My interest in the subject sparked from an early age; I have always used technology and have been fascinated by the underlying genius behind everyday tools like smartphones and computers. From building my first few programs using C it gave me a glimpse into the possibilities of programming. After education Im persuing a job in software development where I can build creative, compelling front-end applications or develop and maintain databases on backend data-centric applications.
